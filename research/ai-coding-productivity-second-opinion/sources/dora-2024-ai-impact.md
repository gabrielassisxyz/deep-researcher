# DORA 2024 AI Impact

## Source Type

Industry research report from DORA, a Google Cloud program. It is survey-based and model-based rather than a controlled coding-task experiment.

## Key Evidence

- DORA reports AI adoption is associated with higher individual productivity, flow, and job satisfaction.
- DORA also reports trade-offs: a 25% increase in AI adoption is associated with a 1.5% decrease in delivery throughput and a 7.2% decrease in delivery stability.
- The report page says AI adoption increases code review speed and code quality in self-reported or modeled dimensions, while Google Cloud's summary stresses that delivery performance can worsen.
- DORA recommends local baseline measurement and experimentation rather than assuming AI adoption will automatically improve delivery.

## Caveats

The report is not an RCT and cannot settle causality for a team. It uses broad organizational survey data and associations.

## Practical Implication

DORA supports the pattern that individual experience can improve while system-level delivery metrics do not necessarily improve and may worsen.
