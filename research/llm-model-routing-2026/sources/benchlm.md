# BenchLM Leaderboard

## Summary

BenchLM tracks hundreds of models across many benchmarks, pricing, context, and runtime data. It distinguishes provisional-ranked and verified-ranked models, assigns confidence indicators based on sourced benchmark coverage, and reports category weights for agentic, coding, reasoning, multimodal, knowledge, multilingual, instruction-following, and math tasks.

- BenchLM ranks Gemini 3.1 Pro, Claude Fable 5, Claude Opus 4.8, GPT-5.5, Gemini 3.5 Flash, DeepSeek V4 Pro, GLM-5.2, and other requested models in a unified table.

## Limitations
