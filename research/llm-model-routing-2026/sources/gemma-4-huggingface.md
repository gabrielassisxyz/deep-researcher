# Gemma 4 Hugging Face Model Cards

## Summary

Google's Gemma 4 Hugging Face model cards describe Gemma 4 as open models built by Google DeepMind. They verify multimodal text and image input with text output, audio support on smaller E2B and E4B models, Apache 2.0 licensing, model sizes, 128K to 256K context, and benchmark tables for reasoning, coding, vision, audio, and long-context tasks.

## Claims Used

- Gemma 4 is an open model family under Apache 2.0.
- Gemma 4 includes E2B, E4B, 26B A4B, and 31B variants.
- Gemma 4 31B has 30.7B parameters and 256K context.
- Gemma 4 26B A4B has 25.2B total parameters, 3.8B active parameters, and 256K context.
- Reported vendor benchmarks include LiveCodeBench v6: 80.0% for 31B and 77.1% for 26B A4B; MMLU Pro: 85.2% and 82.6%; Codeforces ELO: 2150 and 1718.
- Core capabilities include thinking, image understanding, video understanding, function calling, coding, multilingual use, and audio for E2B/E4B.

## Limitations

Benchmark tables are model-card claims by the model publisher. They are valuable, but the routing guide treats them as primary vendor evidence rather than independent benchmark confirmation.
